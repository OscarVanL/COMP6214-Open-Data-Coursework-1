Introduction

This page features several visualisations of the Business Impacts of COVID-19 Survey (BICS) dataset collected by the Office for National Statistics.

Several sheets from the BICS Wave 4 survey are shown, including the sample size, response rate, business trading status, and businesses participation in COVID-related government initiatives.

Data relates to the period 06/04/2020-19/04/2020.
